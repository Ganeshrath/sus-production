4. ACCESSIBILITY SERVICE DISCLOSURE

MindLock uses Android's Accessibility Service. Because this is a sensitive permission, we are fully transparent about its use.

What the Accessibility Service Does

App Blocking Service (MindLock — App Blocker)

  • Detects which app is currently in the foreground (which app you just opened)
  • Detects when you are in a short-video section (Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, TikTok)
  • Counts scroll events when you are in short-video sections
  • Detects when you navigate to settings pages that could be used to bypass MindLock (only during Strict Mode)

Content Filter Service (MindLock — Content Filter)

  • Detects when a browser is open in incognito/private mode (by reading the window class name only)
  • This service does NOT read URL bar content, page content, or any text you type

What the Accessibility Service Does NOT Do

  • Does not read passwords, PINs, banking details, or any text you type
  • Does not read the content of messages, emails, or any communication
  • Does not capture screenshots or record your screen
  • Does not read page content of any website
  • Does not transmit any accessibility event data outside your device
  • Does not remain active when payment or banking apps are open

Why We Need It

Android does not provide a public API to detect the foreground app in real time without using either Accessibility Service or UsageStats polling. We use Accessibility Service because it allows us to detect the precise moment an app opens, which is necessary for the breathing/intent flow in Utility 1 (Per-App Interception) and doom scroll detection in real time.

Your Control

You can disable either or both accessibility services at any time via: Settings → Accessibility → Installed Services → MindLock. Disabling the App Blocker service will stop all app blocking functionality. The Content Filter VPN will continue working for domain-level blocking.

6. LOCAL VPN AND CONTENT FILTERING

MindLock's Content Filter feature uses a local VPN on your device to filter DNS requests.

Important facts about this VPN:

  • It is a local VPN that runs entirely on your device
  • It does not connect to any external VPN server
  • It does not route your internet traffic through any third-party server
  • It does not make you anonymous or hide your IP address
  • Its only function is to block DNS requests to adult content domains
  • All DNS queries are forwarded to Cloudflare Family DNS (1.1.1.3) — a publicly available, privacy-respecting DNS resolver that blocks adult content categories

What the VPN does not do:

  • Does not log which websites you visit
  • Does not transmit your browsing data to us or any third party
  • Does not affect your internet speed significantly
  • Does not access the content of any HTTPS connections

The local VPN is completely optional. You can disable it at any time from MindLock Settings → Content Filter → toggle OFF.
