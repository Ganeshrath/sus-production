2. DESCRIPTION OF SERVICE

MindLock is a digital wellness Android application that provides the following services:

  • App Blocking: Intercepting and adding friction to the opening of user-selected distracting apps
  • Focus Mode: Temporarily locking user-selected apps for a defined time period
  • Doom Scroll Guard: Detecting and interrupting continuous short-video scrolling
  • Challenge System: Structured detox challenges with XP rewards and streak tracking
  • Content Filter: Local VPN-based DNS filtering to block adult content websites
  • Incognito Blocking: Detecting and blocking private/incognito browser modes
  • Strict Lock Mode: Preventing changes to app settings for a user-defined period
  • Brain Health Visualization: Graphical representation of daily digital wellness based on usage patterns
  • Usage Statistics: Detailed breakdown of daily app usage and attention metrics
  • Scheduled Blocking: Time-based automatic blocking of app categories
